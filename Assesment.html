Algorithmic Dependence
A Self-Assessment
Companion Tool · 2025

Are you using your devices, or are they using you?

A 20-question reflective assessment based on the empirical thresholds of harm identified in the research paper "From Mindful Living to Algorithmic Dependence."

This self-assessment translates the diagnostic criteria from the research paper into a short, structured set of questions across three domains: self-tracking behaviour, AI-companion reliance, and cognitive offloading.

It takes about four minutes. Your answers are processed only in your own browser — nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored, nothing is shared.

Based on the research of
Hiya Kashyap & Shivi
Department of Computer Science and Engineering · Chitkara University, Punjab
Supervised by Dr. Rajat Takkar
A Note Before You Begin This tool is designed to support reflection and self-awareness. It is not a clinical diagnostic instrument. If your answers raise concerns, consider consulting a qualified mental-health professional.
